Purely Platonic

Currently in progress


With the current global pandemic, people who are isolated or have moved to a new community are left without friendships or social connections. Due to the implementation of social distancing and safety concerns, it has been increasingly challenging to meet new people comfortably. Even prior to implementing COVID-19 regulations, people struggled to find friends with similar interests, making it difficult for them to foster a deeper sense of connection. The web application we are currently developing will give users the opportunity to find a supportive community of people based on their interests and hobbies
